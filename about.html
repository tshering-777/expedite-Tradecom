Briefly About The Company

In Thimphu, Bhutan, there is a trading firm called Experdite TradeCom. In order to consistently surpass expectations and set new ones, we built stronger and longer-lasting working connections with our customers and suppliers. With the help of our partners, we organize and manage foreign projects, import and export items, and establish business flows.
We are able to integrate all these functions with our successful experience thanks to the support of our network. We can respond to the demands of industries searching for a new market thanks to our wide range of expertise. We are eager to have you join our organization and work with you to expand and improve your current business in Thimphu.
